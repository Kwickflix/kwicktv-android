# Kwick Player — Android

Download the KwickTV Android apps and see what's new in every version.

## Download

Grab the latest APK from the **[Releases page](../../releases/latest)**.

| Edition | Who it's for | File |
|---|---|---|
| **KwickTV Player** | KwickTV members — sign in with your KwickTV username & password | `KwickTV-PRIVATE-*.apk` |
| **Kwick Player** | Everyone — bring your own Xtream Codes or M3U provider | `KwickPlayer-PUBLIC-*.apk` |

> **Kwick Player** is also on Google Play (search "Kwick Player") — the Play
> version updates itself automatically. The APKs here are for sideloading on
> Fire TV, Android TV boxes, and phones.

## How to install (sideload)

**Phone / tablet:** download the APK, tap it, allow "install from this
source" if asked.

**Fire TV / Android TV:** use the **Downloader** app and enter the code:
- KwickTV Player (members): **4054731**
- Kwick Player (public): **6164694**
